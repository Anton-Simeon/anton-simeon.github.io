Design: Convenient. Laconic. Stylish.

The site should fulfill the assigned tasks, be convenient, simple and aesthetically pleasing. Therefore, when creating a design, I pay special attention to the structure, interaction logic, typography, and composition. I remove the unnecessary elements and focus on straightforward, minimalistic, and, most importantly, effective solutions.

How I work

Good results require hard work and proper consistency. That is why my whole development process happens in stages:

  • Briefing
  • Analytics
  • Prototyping
  • Concept
  • Development of the mockups
  • Transfer of layouts to development

Nothing will be missed or forgotten: each stage is broken down into smaller pieces, point-by-point, step-by-step. Final goal: Figma file with adaptive pages.

Collecting data. Analyzing. Reproducing.

Thinking, not just drawing. The maximum can only be achieved by thoroughly studying everything: I immerse myself in the client's working atmosphere, research the market, analyze competitors, define the target audience, monitor industry trends, and get straight to work.
