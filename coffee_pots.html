About

Online store for the Canadian company which produces energetic snacks as an alternative to usual drinking coffee - Chocolate Covered Coffee Beans.

Project Goals
This project aimed to provide coffee enthusiasts with a seamless shopping experience while highlighting the exceptional quality and unique flavors of their products. Create an intuitive and visually appealing website that guides visitors from exploration to purchase with ease. Of course, the website is fully responsive across all devices to reach a broader audience.

Result of work
I implemented an intuitive navigation system that allows customers to effortlessly browse through various coffee blends, explore detailed product information, and add items to their shopping cart. Therefore, this project exemplifies my commitment to creating visually simple and stylish, user-friendly, and results-driven online stores.
