About the project

The Anchor Appart website project is a club house concept for a luxury real estate developer. Landing page design is supposed to look prestigious, but restrained and stylish at the same time. In the process of working on the project, competitors were worked out, a visual analysis was made, the site structure and a functional prototype were created. After drawing the layout, the front-end part was implemented.
