Design: Convenient. Laconic. Stylish.

The site should fulfill the assigned tasks, be convenient, simple and aesthetic. Therefore, when designing a design, I pay special attention to structure, interaction logic, typography and composition. We remove all unnecessary, leave only the necessary, therefore the choice for minimalistic solutions.

How I work

Good results require hard work and proper consistency. That is why my whole development process happens in stages:

  • Briefing
  • Analytics
  • Prototyping
  • Concept
  • Development of the mockups
  • Transfer of layouts to development

Do not forget anything, do not miss: each stage is also broken down separately, point by point, all in order, step by step. Bottom line: Figma file with page adaptives.

Collecting data. Analyzing. Reproducing.

Thinking, not just drawing. The maximum can only be achieved
by thoroughly studying everything: we immerse ourselves in the client's working atmosphere, research the market, analyze competitors, determine the target audience, be relevant: monitor industry trends and start developing.
